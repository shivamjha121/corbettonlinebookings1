About Us

❄ Jim Corbett National Park !!

Welcome to Corbett Travel Packages, your gateway to the untamed and awe-inspiring beauty of Jim Corbett National Park. As the first national park in India, Corbett National Park holds a special place in the hearts of wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Established on August 8, 1936, as Hailey National Park, it was later renamed Corbett National Park in 1957 in honor of the renowned British-Indian hunter, conservationist, and author, Jim Corbett.Jim Corbett National Park is a haven for wildlife, boasting a rich biodiversity that includes the majestic Bengal tiger.

At Corbett Travel Packages, we offer you the opportunity to explore the diverse and exotic landscapes of the Corbett Tiger Reserve. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Jim Corbett National Park is a haven for wildlife, boasting a rich biodiversity that includes the majestic Bengal tiger. Our guided safaris, conducted every morning and evening by expert tourist guides and naturalists, provide you with a chance to witness the fascinating behavior of wild animals and the breathtaking beauty of nature.

Our Corbett National Park tours are known for a variety of safari rides, including elephant safaris and jeep safaris. These adventures are not only thrilling but also educational, allowing wildlife enthusiasts to learn about different activities of the region's unique flora and fauna. If you're a wildlife lover, Jim Corbett National Park is the ideal destination to explore the incredible wildlife of India.
